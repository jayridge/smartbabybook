Did you enjoy your pregnancy?

1 of 6

  • It was marvelous. I loved it. I loved getting fat. I loved feeling this huge belly and her kicking and feeling her different bones sticking out—watching her go back and forth. I felt special. I was getting attention. We were trying to get the house finished. We were working on Mary’s room—painting and decorating. It was fun.

    Mary’s mother

  • I know some women feel like it was the most special time of their lives. But I didn’t feel that way. I was really happy about being pregnant, but I was miserable being pregnant. I just felt terrible. I was always tired. I could get ten hours of sleep an still be exhausted. And I ached—my muscles ached. I felt like I had the flu all the time.

    Jared’s mother

  • I didn’t really enjoy the pregnancy that much. It was a complete turnaround in my life style. All of a sudden I was totally concerned—I gave up drinking, coffee, going out to parties, smoking cigarettes—a lot of things. I stopped working. We didn’t go away anywhere because I just didn’t feel right in traveling. I guess I was being overly cautious about a lot of things.

    Erica’s mother
